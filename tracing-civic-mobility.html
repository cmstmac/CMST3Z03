mdi-mapWhat is GPS Drawing?

Also known as GPS art, GPS drawing is a method where an artist uses a Global Positioning System (GPS) device to follow a pre-planned route and create a large-scale picture or pattern. The .GPX data recorded during the drawing process is visualized as a line on a map. Artists often run or cycle the route, while cars, boats, or planes can be used for larger artworks. Learn more.
